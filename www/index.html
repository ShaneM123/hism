Begin connecting your Neighbourhood
Through close knit cooperation

Trade Items with your community and avoid long and dangerous trips to town

Our trading system allows community keep track of their local inventory. Those in need can request items while those with large surplus can provide and help their community.

No more running out of toilet paper. Hism allows it's user to closely monitor supply and logistics of their near-by area. Every aspect of hism is opt-in and information is shared with your privacy in mind.

Are you looking for toilet paper but are severely lacking in eggs for your omelet? cant find any at the store? Dont worry, hism has you covered. simply select from the neighbourhood section and start managing you inventory today.
